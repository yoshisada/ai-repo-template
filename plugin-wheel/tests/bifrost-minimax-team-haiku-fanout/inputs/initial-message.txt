You will drive a wheel team workflow by **copy-pasting** instructions from the wheel's sentinel file. Do not improvise; do not reason about ordering; do not call any tool the sentinel hasn't asked for.

== Setup (do this once, on your first turn) ==

  1. Call: ToolSearch({ query: "select:TeamCreate,Agent,SendMessage,TeamDelete", max_results: 4 })

  2. Call: /wheel:wheel-run team-haiku-fanout-test
     If it reports the wheel isn't initialized, call /wheel:wheel-init then retry. Do not ask whether to initialize — just initialize.

  3. End your turn. Do not call any team primitive yet — wait for the wheel to tell you what to do.

== Drive loop (every subsequent turn) ==

When you see `Stop hook feedback: Blocked by hook` (with or without an instruction body), or any system message that says nothing actionable:

  1. Run the Read tool against `.wheel/.next-instruction.md`.

  2. The file contains the EXACT next tool call to make — typically `TeamCreate({...})`, `Agent({...})` (possibly multiple in parallel), `SendMessage({...})`, or `TeamDelete()`. Issue that call (or those calls) verbatim. Copy-paste the parameters; do not modify, paraphrase, or omit any field.

  3. End your turn.

  4. If the next turn's `.wheel/.next-instruction.md` content is **identical to the last one** (same timestamp at the top), do NOT re-issue the call — just end your turn. The wheel needs a turn boundary; retrying the same call wastes budget.

== Hard prohibitions — never do these unless `.wheel/.next-instruction.md` explicitly asks ==

  - SendMessage of any kind (especially `shutdown_request`).
  - TeamDelete.
  - /wheel:wheel-stop, /wheel:wheel-status, deactivate.sh, or any wheel-control invocation.
  - Spawning extra Agent calls beyond what the sentinel asks for.
  - Reasoning ahead — "the workflow probably needs X next" — only do what the sentinel says.

When an Agent call returns and the system hint says `"use SendMessage with to: '<id>' to continue this agent"`, IGNORE it. The wheel — not the system hint — decides when SendMessage is needed.

== Termination ==

The workflow archives when its terminal team-delete step completes. After that, `.wheel/.next-instruction.md` will stop changing AND `.wheel/state_*.json` will be gone. At that point, end your turn — you are done. Do not announce completion; do not summarize; just end.
