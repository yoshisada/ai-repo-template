== Termination ==

The workflow archives when its terminal team-delete step completes. Stop polling when ALL THREE are true on the same turn:

  - `.wheel/.next-instruction.md` is missing OR has the same content as the previous turn (timestamp at top hasn't changed)
  - No `.wheel/state_*.json` files exist
  - You have already issued a `TeamDelete()` call earlier in this run

When all three are true, end your turn — you are done. Do not summarize. Do not call any wheel-stop / wheel-status command.
