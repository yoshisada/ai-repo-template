# Feedback: planning surface is a single-line scratchpad

## What

`/kiln:kiln-roadmap` currently appends one-line bullets to `.kiln/roadmap.md`. That's not a planning surface — it's a graveyard. Feedback, critiques, and research intents get flattened into the same undifferentiated list.

## Why it matters

Items with different shapes (a feature to build vs a critique to disprove) need different capture and different downstream flows. A single-line list can't carry that.
